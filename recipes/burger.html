Description

I'll keep this one brief. This is a recipe for a burger. No fluff, just tasty.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Kaiser Roll
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup
  • Mayo
  • Greek Yogurt

Steps

  1. Preheat Cast Iron to medium heat.
  2. Form the Beef Patties.
  3. Put the patties on the cast iron once preheated.
  4. Cook on that side without touching for 2.5 to 3 minutes.
  5. While Cooking, do the following:
    • Toast Buns.
    • Salt top side of patty.
    • Squirt some mustard on top side of patty.
  6. After Timer is finished, flip patties and salt recently cooked side to taste.
  7. Cook on that side without touching for 2 minutes.
  8. After timer is complete, take patties off and place directly on toasted buns.
  9. Top with mayo and ketchup to taste.
  10. Cover top bun with Greek Yogurt.
  11. Enjoy!
