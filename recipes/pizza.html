Recipe for Pizza

Description

Although I'm sure you'd love to have a Pizza from the amazing country in Italy, not all of us can afford that trip.

This Pizza recipe is for those of us who dream of something better, or are just fine with a simple homemade pizza.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Protein of Choice
  • Mozzarella
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Let pizza dough rise for a couple hours in a warm and humid location.
  2. After dough has risen, preheat oven to 425 degrees fahrenheit.
  3. Shape dough into desired pizza shape and bake for about 5 minutes.
  4. Take pizza dough/crust out for dressing.
  5. In this order, layer on pizza sauce, basil, protein, then cheese. Make sure to spread things evenly.
  6. Place fully dressed pizza back in oven for an additional 12 to 17 minutes. Check every 2 minutes after the 10 minute mark.
  7. Remove pizza when cheese is melted/browned to personal preference.
  8. Let pizza cool on counter for about 5 minutes then cut.
  9. Enjoy!
